Caramel

Description

A sweet treat made out of sugar, water, butter and cream

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Heat up half the amount of sugar to the ammount of water (200ml water = 100g sugar)
  2. Let it become brown - but watch out! Do not let it burn!
  3. Take pan of the stove
  4. Melt butter inside
  5. Add cream and stir until everything is homogenous
  6. Serve and enjoy!
