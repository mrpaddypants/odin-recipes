Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

An Italian pasta dish with minced meat and tomato sauce.

Famously invented in Bologna, Italy.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Minced beef
  • Minced pork
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onions
  • Tomato paste
  • Red wine
  • Beef stock
  • Milk or cream
  • Parmiggano Regiano
  • Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. Cut up carrots, celery and onions
  2. Sweat them in a medium-sized pan
  3. Add minced meat
  4. After the meat is cooked through, add tomato paste and let it cook a bit in the fat
  5. Deglaze the pan with the red wine and let the alcohol evaporate
  6. Add beef stock and let everything simmer on low heat
  7. Cook spaghetti in boiling, heavily salted water (follow package instructions)
  8. Serve with freshly grated parmigiano reggiano and a small basil leaf
  9. Enjoy!
